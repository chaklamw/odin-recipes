How to Make Fried Rice

Homepage

Description

If your hungry and need something to fill you up, fried rice can do exactly that! It's a perfect lunch and dinner option as it has carbs, vegetables and protein. It won't take long to prepare either. However, it should be noted that this is not an everyday dish.

Ingredients

  • Cold Day Old Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chicken or other choice of meat
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables of choice
  • Oil of choice

Directions

  1. Heat up oil in pan and then put the eggs in it
  2. Scramble eggs, once scrambled separate it from the pan
  3. Cook vegetables and meats of choice, separate when cooked
  4. Combine the rice, vegetables, meat and eggs into thhe pan
  5. Flavor with oyster and soy sauce until it suits your taste
  6. Once suited to taste, turn off the heat and serve
