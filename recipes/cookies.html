How to Bake Cookies

Description

A delicious treat for both family and friends! Note that this is not the only way to make cookies. You can add protein powder, chocolate chips or any other sort of topping. This kind of treat is best paired along with some milk. Expect this to take over an hour.

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons unsalted room temperature butter
  • 85 grams of granualted sugar
  • 71 grams of light brown or dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 180g of all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of milk (optional)
