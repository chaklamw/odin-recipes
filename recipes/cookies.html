How to Bake Cookies

Homepage

Description

A delicious treat for both family and friends! Note that this is not the only way to make cookies. You can add protein powder, chocolate chips or any other sort of topping. This kind of treat is best paired along with some milk. Expect this to take over an hour.

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons unsalted room temperature butter
  • 85 grams of granualted sugar
  • 71 grams of light brown or dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 180g of all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Ligthly grease two baking sheets or line up with parchment paper
  3. Beat together the butter, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda
  4. Add the egg, beating until smooth
  5. Add the flour, mixing completely
  6. OPTIONAL STEP: if adding nuts or chips, beat in the milk first and then the addons, otherwise omit the milk
  7. Drop the cookie dough by the tablespoon onto the baking sheets, spacing them two inches from each other
  8. Bake the cookies for 14-16 minutes, shorter time for softer cookies
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool
  10. Enjoy!
