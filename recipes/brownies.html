How to Bake Brownies

Homepage

Description

A delicious sweet treat! It can be soft or crunchy depending on how you make it. Personally, I like it soft.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Grease a 9x13 inch pan
  3. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt into a large bowl until combined
  4. Spread the mixture into the greased 9x13 inch pan
  5. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes, until the top is dry
  6. Let cool and serve
